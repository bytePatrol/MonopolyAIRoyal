Personality Types

Each personality type influences how the AI approaches the game:

Aggressive

Buys properties when cash is above 50% of price. Builds houses aggressively. Takes high-risk, high-reward moves. Dominates through rapid expansion.

Conservative

Only buys when holding 2x the property price. Maintains large cash reserves. Avoids risky trades. Wins through financial stability and patience.

Mathematical

Calculates expected values for every decision. Uses Monte Carlo-style reasoning. Optimizes for long-term ROI. Data-driven play style.

Trade Shark

Prioritizes trades and negotiation. Seeks leverage through strategic property acquisition. Builds monopolies through shrewd dealing.

Chaotic Evil

Unpredictable and volatile. Takes extreme risks. Makes surprising moves that can either dominate the game or lead to early bankruptcy.

Balanced

Steady middle-ground approach. Adapts to the situation. No extreme tendencies. Wins through consistent, reliable decision-making.

200+ Available Models

Through OpenRouter, you can assign any of 200+ models to custom players, including:

  • Anthropic: Claude 3.5 Sonnet, Claude 3 Opus, Claude 3 Haiku
  • OpenAI: GPT-4 Turbo, GPT-4o, GPT-3.5 Turbo
  • Google: Gemini 2.0 Flash, Gemini Pro 1.5
  • DeepSeek: DeepSeek V3, DeepSeek Coder
  • Meta: LLaMA 3 8B, 70B, 405B
  • Mistral: Mistral Large, Mixtral 8x7B
  • And many more — browse in Admin > Models
Model costs vary significantly. Gemini Flash is among the cheapest ($0.10/1M input tokens), while GPT-4 Turbo is the most expensive ($10/1M input tokens). Check the Budgets section for cost management.
