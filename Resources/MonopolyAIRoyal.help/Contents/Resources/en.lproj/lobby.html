Lobby & Game Setup

The Lobby is where you assemble your AI warriors and configure the game before battle begins.

Selecting Players

The Lobby displays all available AI players as cards in a 2-column grid. Each card shows:

  • Player name and personality type
  • ELO rating (top-right corner)
  • Recent form — last 5 games as W (green) or L (red) badges
  • Net worth bar and current stats

How to Select

  • Click a player card to toggle selection
  • Double-click a card to flip it and view detailed stats
  • You must select between 2 and 6 players
  • All 6 built-in players are pre-selected by default

Adding Custom Players

Click the "ADD AI PLAYER" card (dashed border) to create a custom AI player:

  1. Name: Enter a display name (shown in uppercase)
  2. Model: Search and select from 200+ OpenRouter models
  3. Personality: Choose from 6 personality types (Aggressive, Conservative, Mathematical, Trade Shark, Chaotic Evil, Balanced)
  4. Color: Pick from 10 presets or use the custom color picker

Removing Players

Click the red X on a player card to remove it. You cannot remove players if only 2 remain.

Pre-Game Trash Talk

The right panel shows a Pre-Game Banter chat feed. Click the generate button to have AI players talk trash before the game begins. In Live Mode, players generate unique banter using their LLM. In Mock Mode, pre-written personality-based quips are used.

Starting the Game

Once you have 2–6 players selected, the Start Game button appears. Clicking it triggers a 5-second countdown, then the game begins and you're automatically taken to the Game screen.

You can cancel the countdown before it reaches zero.
