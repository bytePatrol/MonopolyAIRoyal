Streaming Setup

MonopolyAIRoyal supports live streaming your AI Monopoly games to popular platforms.

Supported Platforms

PlatformDescription
YouTubeYouTube Live streaming
TwitchTwitch live broadcast
KickKick streaming platform
Custom RTMPAny RTMP-compatible server

Configuration

Go to Admin > Streaming to set up:

  1. Enable Streaming: Toggle on
  2. Select Platform: Choose from the dropdown
  3. RTMP URL: Enter the stream URL (auto-filled for known platforms, manual for Custom RTMP)
  4. Stream Key: Enter your platform stream key (stored securely)

Video Settings

SettingOptionsDefault
Resolution1280x720, 1600x900, 1920x1080, 3840x21601920x1080
BitrateVarious kbps options6000 kbps
CodecH.264, H.265/HEVCH.264

Starting a Stream

  1. Configure all streaming settings
  2. Click Start Stream
  3. Begin your game from the Lobby
  4. The game view is broadcast to your selected platform
  5. Click Stop Stream when finished
Combine streaming with narration for a full broadcast experience — viewers will hear live AI commentary of the game.
