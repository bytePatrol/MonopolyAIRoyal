Post-Game

After a game ends, the Post-Game screen presents a comprehensive breakdown of the match with highlights, standings, and AI player interviews.

Victory Banner

The top of the screen displays a celebratory banner with confetti animation announcing the winner, including:

  • Winner's name and avatar
  • Trophy icon
  • Final turn count
  • Game duration

Top Game Moments

Five highlighted moments from the game, each showing turn number, title, description, and amount:

MomentDescription
Power MoveA significant property acquisition or strategic play
Rent TsunamiA massive rent payment that shifted the game
Knockout!A player bankruptcy event
Deal of the CenturyA particularly favorable trade
Last StandA dramatic survival moment or close call

AI Player Interviews

Post-game quotes from each AI player, role-based on their final standing:

  • Winner: Victory speech reflecting their personality
  • Runner-up: Second-place reflection
  • First Out: The first player eliminated shares their thoughts

Quotes are tailored to each personality type — aggressive players boast about dominance, mathematical players cite statistics, chaotic players revel in the chaos.

Final Standings

A ranked table of all players with comprehensive stats:

Actions

Play Again

Returns to the Lobby with the same players pre-selected. Click Start to begin a new game immediately.

View Full Stats

Transitions to the Stats screen for deeper analytics, ELO trends, and cross-game data.
